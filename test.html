Who are we

We are artisans traders who each year come together on Christmas markets in France to sell our creations. This difficult year has put our commitments on hold. The decreased activity was morally and financially hard for all of us. The importance of the end of the year celebrations is great for everyone, which is why we want, through our values, to bring balm to your heart. We have therefore created this platform to allow you to overcome this situation, to prepare your reunion with your family and thus end the year in style. We offer you to buy our artisanal products for Christmas on our website.
